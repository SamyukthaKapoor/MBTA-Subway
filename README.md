# MBTA-Subway

[MBTA website Link](https://sites.google.com/view/ie6700group9/home)

Explore the data-driven narrative of Boston's MBTA Subway system! This repository houses visualizations created with Flourish, Tableau, and Datawrapper, offering dynamic insights into daily operations, ridership patterns, and the iconic Red, Green, Blue, and Orange Lines. Join us on a journey through Boston's transit landscape!


Key Features:

Daily Operations: Witness the daily hustle from 5 AM to 1 AM, shaping the commute experiences of millions of Bostonians.
Tech and Academic Nexus: Serving as the essential transit network for Boston's renowned academic and tech institutions — Northeastern, BU, Harvard, and MIT — the MBTA is a reliable companion for students and professionals navigating the city's vibrant landscape.
Dynamic Visualizations:

Flourish, Tableau, Datawrapper: Explore our interactive visualizations crafted using Flourish, Tableau, and Datawrapper. Gain insights into ridership patterns, peak hours, and the intricate network of Boston's subway lines.
Innovation Milestones:

Real-time Tracking: In the spirit of innovation, the MBTA embraced real-time tracking in the 1990s and 2000s, redefining the commuter experience.
Iconic Lines: The iconic Red, Green, Blue, and Orange Lines have become synonymous with the city, weaving through neighborhoods and creating a unifying thread in Boston's diverse tapestry.
Future Forward:

Boston-Centric Focus: This website is your window into the heartbeat of Boston's public transit system, providing in-depth visualizations and insights specific to the city.
Accessible Exploration: Designed for both residents and visitors, our user-friendly interface encourages exploration and understanding of Boston's subway network.
